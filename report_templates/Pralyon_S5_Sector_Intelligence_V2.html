Because average sector payment delay already runs 18 days beyond terms, long-credit structures should not be justified off sector growth alone. Liquidity quality matters more than topline growth.

Below-median liquidity and above-median leverage justify conservative exposure sizing versus stronger peers, even when EBITDA margin appears acceptable.

Entities ranking in the lower third of the peer table should be monitored for GST continuity, active charge growth, and collection slippage at a higher frequency.
